# 📊 Customer Insight & Profitability Analysis using the Sample Superstore Dataset

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the **Sample Superstore** dataset. The objective was to transform raw retail transaction data into meaningful business insights by analyzing customer behavior, product performance, regional trends, shipping methods, discount strategies, and profitability.

The project was completed as part of the **DevAlpha Data Analytics Internship Program** and serves as the foundation for subsequent SQL analysis and Power BI dashboard development.


## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare the dataset for analysis.
- Explore customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify profitable and loss-making products.
- Analyze the impact of discounts on profitability.
- Evaluate shipping performance.
- Investigate regional and geographical sales trends.
- Discover business opportunities and operational challenges.
- Generate actionable recommendations based on data.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

Some of the major findings include:

- Technology is the most profitable product category.
- Copiers generate the highest profit among all sub-categories.
- Tables and Bookcases are major contributors to losses.
- Discounts above 30% significantly reduce profitability.
- Standard Class shipping contributes the highest total profit due to order volume.
- First Class shipping generates the highest average profit per order.
- The West region delivers the strongest overall performance.
- Texas was identified as the largest loss-making state despite strong sales.
- Root-cause analysis revealed that a small number of heavily discounted high-value transactions were responsible for many customer-level losses.


## 📊 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Review discount policies for high-value products.
- Increase investment in profitable Technology products.
- Reevaluate pricing strategies for Tables and Bookcases.
- Investigate operational issues in underperforming regions.
- Strengthen relationships with highly profitable customers.
- Monitor discount strategies to improve overall profit margins.
